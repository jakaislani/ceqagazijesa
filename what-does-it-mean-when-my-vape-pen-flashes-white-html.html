What does it mean when the lights on my battery flash red, green, and white?

Typically, this is caused by a connection issue. A small amount of debris can occasionally cause a connection issue, resulting in the pen reaching its maximum capability. In addition, it’s possible that some debris has gotten into the connection between the battery and the charger.

People have also inquired as to why their vape is flashing white.?

A loose battery is frequently the source of this problem. It is not possible for the battery to fully charge if it is not properly screwed into the device, and the indicator light will continue to blink. To correct this problem, unscrew the battery and re-screw it back in to ensure that everything is securely connected.

What does it mean when your vape pen blinks three times, in a similar vein?

G Pen Battery flashing three times indicates a problem in communication between the G Pen Battery and the G Pen Coil, according to standard operating procedure. Make sure that the connection between the two is clean and secure in order to resolve this issue. It is possible that the G Pen Battery has a problem if it blinks three times with each tank or coil.

Also, it’s important to understand what it means when your vape pen blinks.?

If your battery blinks more than 5 times in a row, it indicates that it needs to be recharged immediately. This will occur immediately after any use or inhalation, and it indicates that it is time to recharge your batteries. When screwing in your battery to charge it, the LED will also blink to indicate the charging process is starting.

Why is my Nord blinking white?

8 seconds protection: the white LED light will flash 5 times when vaping for longer than 8 seconds, and the battery will automatically shut down the output. Low voltage protection: when battery capacity is lower than 3.3V, the LED light will flash 15 times and the battery will automatically shut down the output.
