Wiki/Biography

Soundous Moufakir was born on Friday, 17 February 1995 (age 27 years; as of 2022). Her zodiac sign is Aquarius. She is from Morocco, a North African country. According to Soundous, she is of Moroccan and French heritage. In her education she pursued MBA.

Career

As a Financial Analyst

Soundous Moufakir worked as a financial analyst however, she was more inclined towards modelling so she left her job.

As a Model 

Soundous started her modelling career in Mumbai. She worked with brands like Caprese and Softline. She was also part of some bridal shoots.
