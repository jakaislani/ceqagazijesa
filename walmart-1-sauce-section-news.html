The Internet Is Freaking Out Over the New Walmart $1 Sauce Section

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, June 2, 2024

If you haven’t stopped by your local Walmart recently or seen the TikToks, here’s the scoop: Walmart has a brand new section full of mini gourmet sauces that cost only $1 each. The selection is truly elite and I recommend running over there before they sell out.

From what I’ve seen, Walmart features little versions of some of our favorite BBQ sauces (like Kinder and Blues Hog) and hot sauces (like Yellowbird). I’ve also seen tiny bottles of flavored ketchup up for grabs. Fun!

I love this new section of Walmart sauces because it gives you the opportunity to try different products that are normally a little more expensive or harder to find. You don’t have to commit to buying a gigantic jug of something without knowing whether or not you like it. It’s also great for fans of variety and people who get bored easily. Buy one of each sauce so you can keep mixing it up.

These cheap mini sauces also make iconic gifts. If I received a box of fancy, tiny hot sauces on my birthday, I’d freak! They’re so cute and useful. They’d also be perfect for a Christmas stocking. Make a heart out of these on a bed as an anniversary gift. The possibilities are endless!

I hope this Walmart $1 sauce section doesn’t leave us after the holiday season. In fact, I hope they expand it! Walmart: Consider adding some tiny mustard, ranch, or sweet chili sauce to the roster. The internet has shown that we’ll buy any tiny sauces you offer us!
