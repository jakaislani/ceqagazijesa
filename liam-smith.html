Biography

Liam Smith was born in Liverpool, England on July 28, 1988. English boxer who won the WBO Light Middleweight title in October of 2015 by knocking out John Thompson. He’s held multiple titles throughout his career, the first being a Commonwealth Light Middleweight title in 2012 when he was 24. He was scheduled to defend his WBO Light Middleweight belt against Shane Mosley in December of 2015, but contract negotiations fell through. Smith fought, and defeated, Jimmy Kelly instead. He was a successful amateur boxer, winning two ABA Championships (2007, 2008) while fighting for Rotunda ABC.
