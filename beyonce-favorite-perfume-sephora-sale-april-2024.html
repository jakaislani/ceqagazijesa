Beyoncé’s Favorite Angels’ Share Perfume

Kilian Paris Angels’ Share Eau De Parfum

Kilian Paris Angels’ Share Eau De Parfum

Now 20% Off

Angels’ Share is a sweet, warm gourmand scent inspired by founder Kilian Hennessy’s family legacy in the cognac business (yup, that Hennessy family). It features notes of cognac, tonka bean, oakwood, cinnamon essence, praline, and vanilla, making it the perfect seasonal perfume for the current cool spring weather. It’s also fantastic for a night out with your besties or a special date night.

What Our Team Thinks

I’ve been wearing this perfume for the past couple of months and I seriously cannot say enough good things about Angels’ Share. It’s my all-time favorite Kilian Paris fragrance, which is saying a lot because their perfume roster is absolutely sublime. Despite the pricey cost, you only need a small amount of perfume for long-lasting wear. I get compliments from friends and strangers alike whenever I spritz on Angels’ Share. It’s definitely one of the most popular perfumes in my collection, with friends always spraying themselves with it whenever they come over.

Since Kilian Paris rarely holds sales, now is the opportune time to save big on this Beyoncé-approved fragrance before the Sephora event ends on April 15 — or, at least, before the Beyhive causes it to sell out!

Rihanna Also Loves Kilian Perfume

According to our sister site HarpersBAZAAR.com, Bad Gal Riri is known for wearing Kilian’s iconic Love Don’t Be Shy fragrance — which is also on sale at Sephora. So if you’re looking to treat yourself or a lucky loved one to special beauty gifts, you’ll want to add this intoxicating perfume to your Sephora cart, stat!

Kilian Paris Love Don’t Be Shy

Kilian Paris Love Don’t Be Shy

Now 20% Off

Part of Kilian’s Narcotics Collection, this floral gourmand is sweet and sexy just like Rihanna herself. It boasts notes of honeysuckle, rose, orange blossom, and sweet marshmallow, with a warm amber base that keeps your attention.

What Our Team Thinks

Call it the Rihanna effect, but Love Don’t Be Shy was my first introduction to Kilian perfumes (and was my top pick until I added Angels’ Share to my collection). This scent is positively irresistible and adored by fragrance fanatics. It’s soft, sweet, feminine, and feels empowering to wear. I like it for daily wear and appreciate that the aroma lasts 12 hours before I need to touch it up. Many brands have tried to dupe this fragrance, but I can confidently say no one comes close to the original. Do yourself a favor and order a bottle of this iconic fragrance before it sells out. You seriously cannot go wrong with this scent!
