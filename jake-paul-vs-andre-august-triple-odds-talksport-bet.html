August is an American fighter with ten wins, five via the way of knockouts and has one knockout loss on his record as well.

The 35-year-old hasn't faced too many big hitters, though this will be his biggest fight of them all with many more tuning into this fight to see how it plays out.

They will fight in the Caribe Royale in Orlando Florida on Saturday night, around 4am Sunday morning UK time.

August looks ready for the clash this weekend though and clearly with as many knockout wins as he has will pack enough power to cause issues to Paul - who himself has four knockout wins from his seven victories.

Paul's fights have come thick and fast since stepping into professional boxing back in January 2020, mostly taking on former UFC stars with the one exception being Fury.

Paul is the clear odds on favourite though and there's certainly more expectation from many in boxing that he will continue to add to his wins as he progresses early on in his career.
