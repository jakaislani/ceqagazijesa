Nigerian Producer, Rexxie Bags Second Grammy Certificate

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, May 16, 2024

Renowned Nigerian music producer, Ezeh Chisom Faith, known within the showbiz circles as Rexxie has earned for himself another Grammy Certificate.

The versatile producer bagged his certificate after the African Giant, Burna Boy’s ‘Twice As Tall’ studio album won an award for the Best Global Music Album at the 63rd edition of the Grammy Awards in 2021.

Well, the super producer and serial hit-maker has earned a second Grammy Certification for his contribution to Angelique Kidjo’s song, ‘Do Yourself’ off her album, ‘Mother Nature’ which won the Best Global Music Album at the 64th edition of the Grammy Awards.

In Other News; Talented Nigerian musician and songwriter, Joeboy has released the dates for his upcoming American tour.

The tour which he tags as ‘Young Legend Tour’ will commence on May 4 and end on May 30. The tour will see the ‘Alcohol’ hit-maker perform in fifteen different cities across the United States in a space of 4 weeeks.

The cities that will host the empawa signee includes Seattle, Los Angeles, San Francisco, Dallas, Houston, Atlanta, Minneapolis, Las Vegas, DMV, New York, Cleveland, Rhode Island and Philadelphia.
